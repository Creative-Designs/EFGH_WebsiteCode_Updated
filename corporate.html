Key Features of Corporate Telehealth Services

Saves Valuable Time

Access to qualified doctors by phone eradicates time spent languishing in waiting rooms or driving to the doctor’s office.

Saves Money

Telemedicine co-pays are usually much lower than co-pays for office visits. Several telemedicine plans provide no co-payment for a consultation.

Spares the Stress of Visiting a Doctor

Visiting a doctor disturbs daily work. You may require taking time away from work, arranging for a babysitter to take care of your kids and putting off other scheduled activities to go to an in-person appointment.

Solves Healthcare Problems

Conventionally, many of our employee and employer access medical care through face-to- face doctors’ office visits. The physical access is expensive, logistically difficult and time-consuming.Telemedicine eliminates several of these barriers and obstacles.

Key Benefits of Corporate Telehealth Services

Telemedicine Increases Employer Profitability

Money saved indirectly such as from reduced absenteeism and directly such less expensive medical care through telemedicine which increases overall profitability and positively affects your bottom line.

Telemedicine Attract and Retain Employees

For smaller firms that compete for talent and simply provide limited health care benefits, telemedicine is an attractive advantage for employees.

Increase Employee Satisfaction

EFGH is transforming healthcare by offering telehealth services that benefit employees. EFGH makes life easier for your employees by saving them money resulting in a happier and healthier workforce.

Increase Productivity

With telemedicine, employees speedily get help for common colds, allergies, and flu. The faster they get assistance, the less time they spend away from work or unproductively working while sick.

Easy Access to Doctor

Telehealth gives patients easy access to doctors around the clock, permitting employees to make connections via phone, 24 hours a day and seven days of the week. Telehealth services employ intuitive online systems for easy navigation.

Cost Reduction

The suitability of EFGH Telehealth Service saves employers and employees by reducing sitting time in waiting rooms and traveling expenses. It also reduces the necessity to take time off of work to suit an employee’s illnesses into a doctor’s schedule.
