Speciality

Telemedicine designed to meet the needs of all specialties easily.

Primary Care

Primary Care practices involve spending a lot of amount. Asking patients to come into the office for follow-ups is difficult; however, a phone call to patients is not expensive. For an appointment which does not necessitate a physical examination, providing telemedicine solves this problem!

Postoperative Care

To ensure patient safety and provide the high-quality care, EFGH provide high-touch postoperative care. Most especially for women that have undergone a strict process like a C-section, arranging remote appointments allows our doctors to provide that level of care without placing a physical problem on the patient.

Neurology

For patients suffering from movement disorders, traveling to see a neurologist can be a big obstacle to receiving care. EFGH Telemedicine for neurology offers suitable and routine follow-ups for all patients, particularly those burdened by Parkinson’s disease and other movement ailments.

Other Specialties

Regular follow-up and reducing the demand for urgent care facilities and emergency rooms can be accomplished via the strategic use of our telemedicine services.
